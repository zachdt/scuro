# Enum And Constant Labels

## `GameCatalog.ModuleStatus`

| Value | Label | Meaning |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| `0` | `LIVE` | New play and settlement are allowed |
| `1` | `RETIRED` | New play is blocked, settlement remains allowed |
| `2` | `DISABLED` | New play and settlement are blocked |
